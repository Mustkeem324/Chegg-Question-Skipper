Chegg Question Skipper

How it works:

This extension automatically skips questions on Chegg's question authoring page. Once installed, you will see a "Skip Question" button that allows you to bypass questions easily.

How to use:

  • Navigate to the question authoring page on Chegg.
  • Click the "Skip Question" button to automatically skip to the next question.
  • If you want to stop skipping, click the "Stop Skipping" button.
